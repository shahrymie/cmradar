Carbon emission accounting

The methods used in accounting for GHG emissions

  • State the methodology used to calculate GHG emission (e.g. GHG proocol or ISO).
  • Describe the external verification (whom and basis) on GHG emission quantity.
  • Provide evidence of total GHG emissions (metric tonnes of CO2 emitted, cost associated)
  • State the disclosure of direct GHG emissions by Scopes - 1, 2, or 3.
  • Provide evidence of disclosure of GHG emissions by sources ( e.g. coal, electricity, etc.).
  • Provide evidence of GHG emission in comparison with previous years
  • Provide reasons for the changes in level of emission from year to year
